About me

Height - 170 cm

Weight - 62 kg

Body parameters - 94-65-92 cm

Date of Birth - 20.03.2006

I started doing circus at the age of 5, when my parents sent me to the circus studio in my hometown Kharkiv. There I took up working partner acrobatics on ladders and then on cubes. Also I started working with hula-hoops. Later when I was 14 years, I started working group juggling and duo acrobatics.

As an artist, I have experience by taking part in different Ukrainian festivals of circus variety. In most of them I got the first places. From September 2022 I am studying at Circus school of Hungary. My main genre is straps&hula-hoop and also I'm working on silks.
